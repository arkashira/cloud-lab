## 1. Acceptance Criteria
- **Upload/Select Playbook** – The UI/API accepts a playbook file up to **5 MB** and stores it in a sandbox‑specific temporary directory.
- **Execution Target** – The playbook runs against **Terraform‑created instances** only; the runner resolves instance IPs via the sandbox’s Terraform state.
- **Network Isolation** – Any attempt by the playbook to reach an IP outside the sandbox VPC results in an immediate failure and is reported in the output stream.
- **Real‑time Streaming** – The runner streams stdout/stderr line‑by‑line to the client (WebSocket/HTTP streaming) during execution.
- **Result Summary** – After completion, a JSON summary containing `changed`, `ok`, `failed`, and `unreachable` counts is returned.
- **Playbook Validation** – Before execution, the playbook is linted and validated; lint errors prevent execution and are returned to the user.
- **Resource Cleanup** – Temporary playbook files and any spawned processes are cleaned up after execution, regardless of success or failure.
