# Revenue Model

## Pricing Tiers

| Tier          | Price/Seat/Month | Included Quotas                                                                                     | Who Buys This Tier                                                                                     |
|---------------|------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Starter       | $10              | 5 users, 10 AWS instances, 50GB storage, 100 CI/CD pipelines/month                                   | Small teams or startups looking to learn enterprise-grade AWS workflows with basic collaboration.      |
| Professional  | $30              | 20 users, 50 AWS instances, 200GB storage, 500 CI/CD pipelines/month, advanced monitoring            | Mid-sized teams needing more robust infrastructure and collaboration features.                        |
| Enterprise    | $100             | Unlimited users, 200 AWS instances, 1TB storage, unlimited CI/CD pipelines, priority support, SLA    | Large enterprises requiring full-scale enterprise-grade AWS portfolio project templates with SLAs.     |

## Free Tier Limits

- **Users**: 2
- **AWS Instances**: 2
- **Storage**: 10GB
- **CI/CD Pipelines**: 10/month
- **Collaboration Features**: Basic

## Annual/Monthly Split Assumption

- **Annual Subscriptions**: 30% of customers
- **Monthly Subscriptions**: 70% of customers
- **Annual Discount**: 20% off the monthly rate

## Expansion Revenue Path

1. **Seat Growth**: Encourage teams to add more users as they scale their projects.
2. **Usage Growth**: Offer additional AWS instances, storage, and CI/CD pipelines at incremental costs.
3. **Upsell to Higher Tiers**: Provide features in higher tiers that encourage users to upgrade for better collaboration and monitoring.

## Anti-Cannibalization

- **Sticky Features**: Higher tiers include advanced monitoring, priority support, and SLAs, making it difficult for customers to downgrade without losing critical functionality.
- **Value Proposition**: The Enterprise tier offers unlimited users and instances, which is essential for large teams, making it a must-have for scaling organizations.
- **Cost Savings**: Annual subscriptions offer significant discounts, incentivizing long-term commitments and reducing the likelihood of downgrading.
